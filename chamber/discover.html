Discover Cordoba

Explore the beautiful Province of Cordoba and learn about our local community, attractions, and businesses.

Local Attractions

Cathedral of Cordoba

The beautiful historic cathedral in the center of Cordoba, dating back to the 16th century.

Lake San Roque

A stunning lake surrounded by mountains, perfect for outdoor activities and relaxation.

Sierras de Cordoba

The magnificent mountain range offering breathtaking views and hiking trails.

Jesuit Block

A UNESCO World Heritage site featuring well-preserved Jesuit buildings from the 17th century.

Capilla del Monte

Famous for its mystical Uritorco Hill and beautiful natural landscapes.

National University of Cordoba

Founded in 1613, it's the oldest university in Argentina with beautiful historic buildings.
